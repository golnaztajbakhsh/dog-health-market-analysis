# Dog Health Market Analysis

Market Potential &amp; Business Simulation Analysis for a Dog Health App using PostgreSQL and Power BI.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the U.S. dog-care market to identify the most promising regions and states for launching a dog health application. The analysis combines demographic, economic, and technology-related indicators to evaluate market size, digital readiness, and monetization potential.

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to identify high-potential markets for a dog health application by analyzing:

* Dog population
* Dog-owning households
* Dog penetration rate
* Technology orientation
* Per capita income

The project aims to transform data into actionable business recommendations for market entry and expansion strategies.

## Dataset

The dataset contains state-level and regional information across the United States, including:

* State and region identifiers
* Population and number of households
* Dog population and dog-owning households
* Per capita income and personal income
* Technology-related indicators
* Calculated KPIs:

  * Dog Penetration Rate (%)
  * Technology Orientation Score

## Tools Used

* PostgreSQL – Data storage, validation, joins, and KPI calculations
* Power BI – Interactive dashboard development and data visualization
* Microsoft Excel – Data inspection and validation
* Git & GitHub – Version control and project documentation

## Key Insights

### South Region

Texas offers the largest market opportunity due to its massive dog population and high number of dog-owning households. The state also demonstrates competitive technology adoption and sufficient income levels to support digital services.

### West Region

California presents one of the strongest opportunities by combining a large pet-owning population, relatively high income levels, and strong technology adoption.

### Northeast Region

The Northeast has the highest average income and technology orientation. Massachusetts stands out as a premium market due to its exceptional digital readiness and purchasing power, while New York provides greater market scale.

### Midwest Region

The Midwest represents a balanced market opportunity. Illinois combines a sizable customer base with good technology orientation and favorable income levels.

## Business Recommendation

The analysis suggests a phased market-entry strategy:

1. **Texas** – Prioritize for initial launch to maximize user acquisition and market reach.
2. **California** – Expand to leverage its balanced combination of market size, income levels, and technology adoption.
3. **Massachusetts** – Target as a premium market characterized by high digital readiness and strong purchasing power.

Overall, Texas provides the largest market scale, California offers the best balance between market size and digital adoption, and Massachusetts represents the most digitally mature and premium market opportunity.
